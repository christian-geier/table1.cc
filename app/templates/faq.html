Q: What is the purpose of table1.cc?
A: Research papers in clinical trials or case-control studies usually include a "Table 1" to summarize population statistics. To minimize error, optimize efficiency and enhance reproducibility, automation of this task is desirable. Currently available software packages for R and Python provide part of the solution but their reliance on programming syntax limits their widespread adoption.
